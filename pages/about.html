Our Story

Who We Are

GT Arsenals Ltd is a privately registered Nigerian technology company based in Abuja, the Federal Capital Territory. We were founded in 2025 with a clear mission: to make quality technology services accessible, affordable, and reliable for every Nigerian — whether you're an individual, a small business, a startup, or a large organization.

From our base in Abuja, we serve clients across FCT and beyond, offering a comprehensive range of services from hardware sales and IT support to software development, cybersecurity, and hands-on skills training.

We believe Nigeria's future is digital, and GT Arsenals exists to play a meaningful role in making that future a reality — one client, one project, and one trained individual at a time.
