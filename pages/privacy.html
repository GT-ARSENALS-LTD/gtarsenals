Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

GT Arsenals Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and website visitors. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Who We Are

GT Arsenals Ltd is a technology company registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria (RC: 8340467), located in Old Secretariat, Garki 1, Abuja, FCT. We operate the website at gtarsenals.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, and phone number (when you contact us or enroll in training)
  • Details about your inquiry or project
  • Payment information (processed securely through trusted payment providers)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser type, device type, and IP address
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referral source (how you found us)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide our services
  • To process training enrollments and payments
  • To send you relevant updates (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only:

  • With service providers who help us run our business (e.g., email providers) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • When required by Nigerian law or a court order
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, including using HTTPS encryption on our website and securing our internal systems. No internet transmission is 100% secure, but we take this seriously and continually improve our practices.

6. WhatsApp Communications

When you contact us via WhatsApp, your messages are handled through WhatsApp's platform. Please refer to WhatsApp's privacy policy for information on how they handle your data. We use WhatsApp for service communication only and do not share your WhatsApp information with third parties.

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, although this may affect some website features.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: info@gtarsenals.com

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with the revised date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
